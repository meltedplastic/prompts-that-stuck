# Learn

**Ask your AI what it noticed about how you work — honestly — so you both get better next time.**

---

## The Prompt

Copy this and paste it at the end of any work session:

```
Based only on this session, tell me:

1. What you noticed about how I think and work
2. What helps me most
3. Where I get stuck
4. How both of us can collaborate better

Be specific, practical, and direct. Reference actual moments from our conversation, not generic advice.
```

---

## When to use this

At the end of a long session — especially after building something, debugging something frustrating, or working through a hard decision with AI.

The first time you try it, you'll be surprised how much your AI picked up that you didn't notice yourself. The more sessions you do this, the more you see your own patterns.

## What surprised me

The AI caught that my first instinct was right every single time — even when I second-guessed myself. That changed how I make decisions now. I trust the gut reaction and use AI to pressure-test it, not to replace it.

---

## Works with

ChatGPT · Claude · Gemini · Any AI with conversation context
